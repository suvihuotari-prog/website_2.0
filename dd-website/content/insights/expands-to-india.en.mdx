Data Design is launching a major initiative in India, establishing new “**Data Design AI Labs **” aimed at fostering innovation and significantly boosting the AI skills of the local workforce. This expansion deepens the growing technology ties between Finland and India, a collaboration highlighted in recent discussions between the countries’ Prime Ministers as vital for future growth.

The new labs are designed as state-of-the-art facilities where students and professionals can dive into practical AI applications. Data Design has announced a significant commitment to train over 10000 professionals through these labs within the next year alone.

## Focus: Developing Tailored AI Solutions for India
The initiative goes beyond just training. Data Design AI Labs will focus on developing cutting-edge AI solutions specifically for India. Key projects include pioneering work on autonomous navigation systems suited for Indian conditions, building custom Large Language Models (LLMs) tailored to the Indian market’s linguistic and cultural nuances, and exploring the potential of advanced Generative AI agents.

## Vision: Empowering Talent and Driving Digital Change
This move represents Data Design’s vision to empower Indian talent to lead the development of localized AI solutions. By bridging the gap between advanced research and real-world implementation, the company aims to nurture local AI expertise and help drive digital transformation across Indian industries.

The establishment of Data Design AI Labs is seen as a concrete step in strengthening the strategic AI partnership between Finland and India, signaling increased collaboration and shared ambitions in the competitive global tech landscape. Data Design is actively seeking collaboration as it embarks on this ambitious project to shape the future of AI in one of the world’s fastest-growing tech markets.
