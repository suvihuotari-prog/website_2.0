## SECTION: The challenge

> **Subtitle:** Data governance sounds boring until its absence blocks every initiative you care about.

### Challenge 1: No clear data ownership
When something goes wrong with data, nobody knows who's responsible. Decisions stall because accountability is unclear.

### Challenge 2: Data quality that erodes trust
Inconsistencies, duplicates, missing values — your people have stopped trusting the numbers. They build their own spreadsheets instead.

### Challenge 3: AI initiatives blocked by data problems
Every AI project starts with 'first we need to fix the data.' Without governance, you're stuck in an endless loop of data preparation.

---

## SECTION: How we solve it

> **Subtitle:** Governance that works means governance people actually use. Our approach is practical, not theoretical.

1. **Current state assessment** — We map your existing data landscape, ownership gaps, quality issues, and the business processes that depend on reliable data.
2. **Ownership & responsibility model** — We define who owns what data — aligned to business processes, not IT systems. Clear roles that make sense to the people who'll carry them.
3. **Quality framework** — Practical quality rules, monitoring, and improvement processes. Metrics your teams can actually follow — not a 200-page policy document.
4. **Adoption & change management** — Governance only works if people use it. We design adoption plans, run training, and embed governance into existing workflows.
