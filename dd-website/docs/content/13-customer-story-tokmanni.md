## SECTION: The challenge
**Background:** White

> **Subtitle:** Tokmanni had a popular loyalty app with millions of users — but standard discount coupons weren't driving the engagement or visits the business needed.

### Challenge 1: Low coupon redemption despite an active loyalty app
Tokmanni's mobile app distributed discount coupons to loyalty customers, but redemption rates stayed low. Generic offers didn't give customers a reason to act.

### Challenge 2: No internal AI or personalization capability
The team lacked the machine learning expertise and infrastructure to build a recommendation engine. Every customer received the same offers regardless of their behavior.

### Challenge 3: No way to match offers to individual preferences
Customer purchase data existed but wasn't being used to personalize the experience. Discounts were broad instead of targeted — costing more and converting less.

---

## SECTION: What we built (Interactive — 4-step process)
**Layout:** Left sidebar step navigation + right animated detail panel
**Background:** Gray

> **Subtitle:** An AI-driven personalization engine that matches the right offer to the right customer — from data analysis through production deployment.

### Step 1: Customer data analysis (The Foundation)
Analyzed purchasing patterns across Tokmanni's loyalty base to understand what drives individual buying decisions and identify high-potential customer segments.

### Step 2: ML model development (The Brain)
Built machine learning models for product recommendations and dynamic per-customer discount pricing — matching the right offer to the right person at the right time.

### Step 3: GenAI-powered product summaries (The Magic)
Used generative AI to create tailored product descriptions for different customer segments, making offers more relevant and engaging.

### Step 4: Integration & gradual rollout (The Scale)
Integrated with existing systems and business rules. A/B testing validated performance before scaling. MLOps infrastructure ensures the models stay accurate over time.
